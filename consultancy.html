Our Work

VivaImpact's consultancy arm partners with social sector actors to strengthen their strategies, systems, and storytelling. We bring practical experience and contextual insight to every assignment.

Our Services

Project Design

We help shape initiatives from scratch, including theories of change, logframes, workplans, and budgets.

Monitoring & Evaluation

Tools, indicators, and analysis to help teams learn, adapt, and demonstrate value.

Policy Development

Tailored documents that reflect legal standards, lived realities, and organizational values.

Proposal Writing

Well-researched, persuasive proposals grounded in evidence and aligned with donor priorities.

Capacity Building

Training and mentorship for staff and partners across key technical and operational areas.

Facilitation

Workshops, planning sessions, or fieldwork? We design and guide processes that are clear and inclusive.
