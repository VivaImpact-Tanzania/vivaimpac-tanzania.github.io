How We Create Impact

At Viva Impact NGO, we believe in the power of communities to drive their own transformation. We provide the tools, knowledge, and support needed to build sustainable change from the ground up. Our approach centers on community-led initiatives, capacity building, and creating lasting systems that empower people to take charge of their own development journey.

Our Mission

Our mission is rooted in the belief that sustainable development comes through collaborative working, knowledge sharing, and continuous learning. We work hand-in-hand with communities to build local capacity, foster leadership, and create systems that promote equity and inclusion.

We are committed to facilitating meaningful change that respects local contexts, cultures, and values while addressing the complex challenges facing communities today. Through collaborative partnerships and evidence-based approaches, we strive to create lasting impact that extends beyond program timelines.

Our Programs

CLEER - Community Leadership and Economic Empowerment Resources

CLEER is our flagship program that builds sustainable economic opportunities through community-led initiatives. This comprehensive program focuses on developing local leadership capacity while creating pathways for economic growth and empowerment.

  • Leadership training and mentorship programs for emerging community leaders
  • Small business development and entrepreneurship support
  • Financial literacy and savings group facilitation
  • Market linkage and value chain development
  • Technology integration and digital literacy training
  • Peer-to-peer learning networks and knowledge sharing platforms

HDHP - Health Dignity and Human Protection

A comprehensive 24-month initiative targeting women and marginalized communities, focusing on health rights, dignity, and protection from violence and discrimination.

  • Sexual and reproductive health education and services
  • Gender-based violence prevention and response
  • Mental health support and counseling services
  • Legal aid and advocacy for health rights
  • Community health worker training and deployment
  • Health system strengthening and policy advocacy

WDP - Water Dignity Power

Water Dignity Power is an innovative program expanding access to clean water, safe sanitation, and hygiene education in Dar es Salaam and surrounding communities, with a special focus on prioritizing women and children.

  • Clean water infrastructure development and maintenance
  • Sanitation facility construction and management
  • Hygiene education and behavior change programs
  • Women's leadership in water management committees
  • Child-friendly WASH facilities in schools and communities
  • Climate resilience and water conservation initiatives
