Who We Are

Viva Impact is a dynamic organization based in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, dedicated to creating meaningful change in our communities. We operate through innovative approaches that combine grassroots community work with professional consulting services.

Our Vision

To be the leading catalyst for positive transformation in East Africa, empowering communities and organizations to achieve sustainable development through innovative solutions and collaborative partnerships.

Our Mission

Our mission is executed through two complementary arms that work in synergy to maximize our impact:

NGO Arm

Working with communities to implement development projects that promote dignity, equity, and opportunity.
