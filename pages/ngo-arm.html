What We Do

The NGO Arm of VivaImpact focuses on addressing real-life challenges through direct community work. Our areas of work include gender equality, livelihoods, WASH, education, and digital inclusion.

Our Approach

We co-create solutions with communities, ensuring every initiative reflects local voices, evidence, and sustainability. We’re not here to impose; we’re here to build together.

Current Focus Areas

Gender Equality

Empowering women and girls through training, mentorship, and safe spaces that promote dignity and agency.

Youth Livelihoods

Providing practical skills, entrepreneurship support, and access to resources for young people.

WASH

Improving access to safe water, sanitation, and hygiene education with a people-first mindset.

Digital Inclusion

Bridging the digital divide through access, training, and technology-based solutions.

Why It Matters

In every project, we ask: is this respectful, sustainable, and useful? If not, we rework it. Our NGO arm is guided by the belief that development must be human, contextual, and fair.
