About Our NGO Work

Through our NGO arm, VivaImpact partners directly with communities to implement transformative projects that address social, economic, and environmental challenges. We focus on solutions that are locally grounded and responsive to real needs.

What We Focus On

  • Gender Justice: Programs that promote dignity, equality, and protection for women and girls.
  • WASH: Improving access to clean water, safe sanitation, and hygiene education.
  • Youth Empowerment: Providing life skills, vocational training, and entrepreneurship support.
  • Digital Inclusion: Equipping communities with digital skills and access to digital opportunities.
  • Climate Resilience: Supporting community-led environmental conservation and adaptation efforts.

How We Work

We co-design our projects with the people we serve. From needs assessments to monitoring results, our approach centers participation, learning, and accountability. We work with local leaders, grassroots groups, and technical experts to ensure relevance and sustainability.

Our Impact Areas

Our current programming is focused in Dar es Salaam, with plans to expand into rural regions across Tanzania. Our team is working on interventions that support young women in vulnerable employment, especially in the informal sector, to access safer, more dignified alternatives.

Interested in Partnering?
